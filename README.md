# CivicData SA

**Open data analyses about San Antonio — made for the people who live here.**

**[civicdatasa.github.io](https://civicdatasa.github.io)**

---

## What This Is
 
CivicData SA is an independent civic analytics project publishing data-driven analyses of San Antonio's public systems, infrastructure, and neighborhoods. The goal is to make open data legible and useful, not just for policymakers, but for anyone who wants to understand the city they live in.
 
We look at questions like: Who has access to transit, green space, and city services? How are resources and attention distributed across neighborhoods? Where are the gaps? The analyses span public transit, land use, equity, and urban infrastructure — with more added over time.

---

## Data Sources

Every analysis on CivicData SA is built entirely on publicly available data. Sources include the City of San Antonio Open Data Portal, VIA Metropolitan Transit, the U.S. Census Bureau, and the National Transit Database. All data, methodology, and code are open and reproducible.
